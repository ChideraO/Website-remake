About 501(See)(Streets)

What started as an idea in a class at New York University has formed into a 501(See)(Streets) nonprofit organization. 501 See Streets works with community groups--ranging from business improvement districts to police precincts to civic and neighborhood associations--to beautify communities, especially those areas known for having high crime rates and graffiti complaints, through art.
